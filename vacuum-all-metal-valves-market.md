<p><ul><li>XHV Valve</li><li>UHV Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum all-metal valves market consists mainly of XHV (Extreme High Vacuum) and UHV (Ultra High Vacuum) valves. XHV valves are designed for applications requiring pressures below 10^-9 Torr, ensuring minimal outgassing and optimal performance in environments like particle accelerators and semiconductor manufacturing. UHV valves operate in pressures ranging from 10^-9 to 10^-6 Torr, suitable for high-precision applications such as research laboratories and vacuum systems. Both types emphasize leak-tight designs and durability, catering to advanced industrial and scientific needs.</p></p>
