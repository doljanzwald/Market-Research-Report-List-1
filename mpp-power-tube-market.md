<p><p>The MPP Power Tube market is categorized into two primary types: Excavation Type and Trenchless Type. Excavation Type involves traditional methods requiring digging trenches to lay power tubes, which can be more labor-intensive and time-consuming. Conversely, Trenchless Type methods utilize technologies like horizontal directional drilling, allowing for the installation of power tubes without extensive surface disruption. This latter approach minimizes environmental impact, reduces restoration costs, and enhances efficiency in urban settings where traditional excavation may be impractical.</p></p>
