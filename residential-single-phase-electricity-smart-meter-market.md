<p><p>The Residential Single Phase Electricity Smart Meter market can be categorized into two main types: IC Card and Non-IC Card meters. IC Card meters utilize integrated circuit technology, allowing users to load credit onto physical cards for energy consumption, promoting user engagement and control. In contrast, Non-IC Card meters operate without cards, relying on direct billing methods or remote monitoring systems, offering convenience and streamlined management for utility providers. Both types enhance energy efficiency and consumption transparency for residential users.</p></p>
