<p><p>The Medical Therapy Chairs market is categorized into three main types: electric, manual, and hydraulic. Electric chairs are powered by motors, offering adjustable positions at the push of a button, enhancing patient comfort and practitioner convenience. Manual chairs require physical effort to adjust, providing a cost-effective option but with limited functionality. Hydraulic chairs utilize a hydraulic system for height adjustments and positioning, offering good stability and ease of use. Each type caters to different healthcare settings, preferences, and budgets.</p></p>
