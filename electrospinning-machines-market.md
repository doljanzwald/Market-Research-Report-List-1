<p><ul><li>Coaxial Electrospinning</li><li>Emulsion Electrospinning</li><li>Melt Electrospinning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrospinning machines market features several key types. Coaxial electrospinning involves two concentric nozzles, enabling the production of structured fibers with core-shell configurations, ideal for drug delivery and tissue engineering. Emulsion electrospinning uses polymer solutions mixed with emulsions to create fibers with unique properties, suitable for applications like filtration. Melt electrospinning, on the other hand, utilizes molten polymers to produce fibers, offering advantages in scalability and mechanical properties, often used in industrial applications such as protective clothing.</p></p>
