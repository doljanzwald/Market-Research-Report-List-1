<p><p>The child safety seats market includes several types designed for various age groups and safety needs. Rearward-facing baby seats provide optimal protection for infants by supporting their neck and spine in a crash. Combination seats serve toddlers by allowing both rearward and forward-facing options as they grow. Forward-facing child seats cater to older children, while high-backed booster seats elevate kids to ensure proper seatbelt positioning. Booster cushions are simpler seat boosters without back support, aimed at older children transitioning to adult seat belts.</p></p>
