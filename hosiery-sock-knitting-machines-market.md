<p><p>The hosiery and sock knitting machines market primarily comprises two types: circular sock knitting machines and flat sock knitting machines. Circular sock knitting machines are designed for high-speed production of seamless socks, utilizing a round needle bed that enables continuous knitting, resulting in efficient output and minimal fabric waste. In contrast, flat sock knitting machines operate on a flat needle bed, producing socks with more intricate designs and patterns but typically at a slower pace. Both types serve distinct manufacturing needs in the textile industry.</p></p>
