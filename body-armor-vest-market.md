In contrast, hard armor comprises rigid plates made from ceramics, metals, or composite materials, designed to withstand higher-caliber threats, including rifle rounds. Each type serves specific needs, balancing protection levels, weight, and mobility for various users, such as law enforcement and military personnel.</p></p>
