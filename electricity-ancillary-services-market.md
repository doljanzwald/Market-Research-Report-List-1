<p><ul><li>Frequency Control Service</li><li>Spinning Reserve Service</li><li>Operating Reserve Service</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electricity ancillary services ensure grid stability and reliability. Frequency control service maintains the desired frequency by adjusting generation or demand in real-time. Spinning reserve service provides backup power that can be quickly activated, using online generators that are not at full output. Operating reserve service includes both spinning and non-spinning reserves, ensuring that additional capacity can be deployed within a specified timeframe to handle unexpected demand spikes or generation losses, thus supporting overall grid integrity.</p></p>
