<p><strong>The Synthetic Bioceramic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydroxyapatite (Hap)</li><li>β-Tricalcium Phosphate (β-TCP)</li><li>Biphasic Calcium Phosphate (BCP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic bioceramic market features several key types. Hydroxyapatite (HAp) is renowned for its similarity to natural bone, promoting excellent biocompatibility and osteoconductivity. β-Tricalcium Phosphate (β-TCP) is biodegradable and enhances bone regeneration through resorption. Biphasic Calcium Phosphate (BCP) combines both HAp and β-TCP, offering a balance of bioactivity and resorption rates, tailored for diverse applications in bone repair and regeneration. Together, these materials drive advancements in orthopedic and dental procedures, catering to specific clinical needs.</p></p>
