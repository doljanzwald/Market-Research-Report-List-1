<p><p>The PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives market is divided into two main types: Non-Reactive and Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives. Non-Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives provide instant bonding and are easy to apply, making them suitable for various applications, including packaging and woodworking. In contrast, Reactive PUR Hot-Melt Adhesives undergo a chemical reaction upon exposure to moisture, leading to stronger and more durable bonds. This type is ideal for demanding applications, such as automotive and construction, where long-lasting adhesion is critical.</p></p>
