<p><p>Styrene Isoprene Styrene (SIS) is a thermoplastic elastomer known for its flexibility and durability, primarily used in adhesives, coatings, and sealants. The market for SIS is categorized into two types: linear and radial. Linear SIS features a straightforward polymer chain structure, providing excellent processability and adhesion properties. In contrast, radial SIS has a star-shaped configuration with multiple arms radiating from a central point, offering enhanced elasticity and performance in demanding applications. Both types cater to various industrial needs, emphasizing their versatility.</p></p>
