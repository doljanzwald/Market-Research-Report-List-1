Medium-pressure systems offer a balance, generating smaller droplets for improved evaporation rates while maintaining energy efficiency. High-pressure mist cooling systems utilize very fine droplets, maximizing evaporation and providing superior cooling effectiveness, making them ideal for large industrial spaces and outdoor areas requiring efficient temperature control.</p></p>
