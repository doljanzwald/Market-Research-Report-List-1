<p><strong>The Inkjet Card Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Foaming</li><li>Micro Piezo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet card printer market features various technologies, including Thermal Foaming and Micro Piezo. Thermal Foaming uses heat to vaporize ink, creating bubbles that propel ink onto the card surface, resulting in vibrant prints. In contrast, Micro Piezo employs piezoelectric crystals to precisely control ink droplet ejection, offering high-resolution images with enhanced detail. Both technologies cater to different user needs, such as speed, quality, and cost-effectiveness, influencing their applications in various industries like identification, membership, and access control.</p></p>
