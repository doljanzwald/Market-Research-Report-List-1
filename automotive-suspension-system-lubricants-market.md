<p><p>The automotive suspension system lubricants market primarily includes two types: lubricating oil for fluid suspension and independent suspension lubricating oil. Lubricating oil for fluid suspension is designed to reduce friction and enhance the performance of hydraulic suspension systems, providing smoother rides and improved handling. Independent suspension lubricating oil, on the other hand, supports the functioning of independent suspension systems, ensuring optimal mobility and stability by minimizing wear and tear on components. Both types play a crucial role in vehicle performance and longevity.</p></p>
