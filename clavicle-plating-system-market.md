<p><p>The Clavicle Plating System market includes two primary types of screw systems: locking screws and non-locking screws. Locking screws provide enhanced stability by securing the plate to the bone, reducing movement at the fracture site and improving healing outcomes. In contrast, non-locking screws allow for flexibility in bone movement, relying on the plate's contour for stability. Both types cater to different surgical preferences and patient needs, impacting overall fixation strength and recovery processes in clavicle fractures.</p></p>
