<p><p>The automotive mechanical fan market consists of various types, primarily clutch fans, flex fans, and others. Flex fans feature flexible blades that adjust their angle based on airflow, providing variable cooling performance. "Others" encompass specialized or less common fan types, which may include viscous fans and dual fans, serving niche applications or specific vehicle requirements, enhancing overall engine performance and cooling efficiency.</p></p>
