<p><p>The Gibberellins market can be categorized into two main types based on their carbon structure: 19-carbon and 20-carbon gibberellins. The 19-carbon gibberellins, primarily synthesized from the fungus Gibberella fujikuroi, are known for their role in promoting seed germination, stem elongation, and flowering. In contrast, 20-carbon gibberellins, derived from various plants, also facilitate growth processes but are more involved in regulating fruit development and enhancing yield. Both types play critical roles in agricultural and horticultural applications, driving demand in the market.</p></p>
