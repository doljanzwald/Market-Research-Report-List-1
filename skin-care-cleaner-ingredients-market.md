<p><ul><li>Anionic Cleaner</li><li>Non-Ionic Cleaner</li><li>Amphoteric Cleaner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin care cleaner ingredients market includes three primary types: anionic, non-ionic, and amphoteric cleaners. Anionic cleaners are negatively charged, providing effective removal of dirt and oils but can be harsh on sensitive skin. Non-ionic cleaners are gentler, suitable for various skin types, and emphasize mild formulations. Amphoteric cleaners, which can act as both anionic and cationic, offer versatility and mildness, making them ideal for sensitive skin formulations. Together, these cleaners cater to diverse consumer needs and preferences in skin care.</p></p>
