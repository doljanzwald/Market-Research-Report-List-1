<p><strong>The Consumer and Office Inkjet Printheads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The consumer and office inkjet printheads market is primarily divided into two types: piezoelectric and thermal. Piezoelectric printheads use mechanical pressure to eject ink droplets, offering precise control and compatibility with a wide range of ink types. In contrast, thermal printheads rely on heat to create vapor bubbles that force ink out. While thermal printheads are commonly used in consumer printers due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, piezoelectric options are favored for high-end applications, providing better quality and versatility.</p></p>
