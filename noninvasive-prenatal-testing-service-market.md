<p><p>Noninvasive Prenatal Testing (NIPT) services are categorized into two main types: NIPT and NIPT Plus. NIPT primarily screens for common chromosomal abnormalities like Down syndrome using a sample of maternal blood. It offers high accuracy with minimal risk. NIPT Plus expands this range, providing additional insights into more rare genetic conditions, enabling deeper genetic profiling of the fetus. Both options appeal to expectant parents seeking reliable information about their baby's health without invasive procedures.</p></p>
