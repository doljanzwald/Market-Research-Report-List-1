<p><p>Bone densitometers (BDMs) are diagnostic devices used to measure bone mineral density, aiding in the diagnosis of osteoporosis and other bone-related conditions. The market for BDMs is primarily segmented into axial BDMs and peripheral BDMs. Axial BDMs, typically found in hospitals, assess bone density in central sites like the spine and hip for comprehensive evaluations. Peripheral BDMs, often portable, measure density in peripheral sites like the wrist or heel, offering convenient screening options and making them suitable for outpatient settings.</p></p>
