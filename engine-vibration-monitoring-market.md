<p><strong>The Engine Vibration Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrator filters (velocity mode)</li><li>Dual-integrator filters (displacement mode)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engine vibration monitoring is critical for assessing the health of machinery. The market is divided into integrator filters and dual-integrator filters. Integrator filters, operating in velocity mode, measure vibrations based on speed of movement, providing insights into dynamic changes. In contrast, dual-integrator filters function in displacement mode, capturing the actual movement or position of engine components. Both types enhance predictive maintenance strategies, minimizing downtime and ensuring optimal engine performance by identifying abnormal vibration patterns that could indicate potential issues.</p></p>
