<p><p>The raw mill market encompasses various types of mills used in cement production, primarily the vertical roller mill and the ball roller mill. Vertical roller mills are favored for their energy efficiency and ability to grind materials to fine powder with minimal noise. In contrast, ball roller mills utilize steel balls to crush and grind raw materials, effective for larger particles and bulk processing. Both types cater to different operational needs, influencing market preferences based on production capacity and energy consumption.</p></p>
