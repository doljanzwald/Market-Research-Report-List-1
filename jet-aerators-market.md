<p><strong>The Jet Aerators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compressed Air Type</li><li>Self-Aspirating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The jet aerators market is primarily segmented into two types: compressed air type and self-aspirating type. Compressed air jet aerators utilize external air compressors to inject air into the water, enhancing oxygen transfer efficiency. In contrast, self-aspirating jet aerators draw ambient air into the water stream through the venturi effect, which combines air and water without the need for a separate air supply. Both types are crucial for improving water treatment processes and ensuring efficient oxygenation in various applications.</p></p>
