<p><ul><li>Lattice Tower</li><li>Guyed Tower</li><li>Monopole Tower</li><li>Stealth Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cellular Tower Management Market comprises several tower types, each serving unique purposes. Lattice towers, characterized by their triangular framework, offer high durability and space for multiple carriers. Guyed towers use cables for support, providing stability for taller structures but requiring significant ground space. Monopole towers are single, streamlined structures ideal for urban areas with limited space. Stealth towers blend into their surroundings, mimicking trees or other structures to minimize visual impact, catering to aesthetic concerns in populated regions.</p></p>
