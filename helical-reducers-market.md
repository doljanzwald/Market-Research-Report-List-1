<p><p>Helical reducers are critical components in machinery, categorized mainly into two types: parallel-axis and perpendicular-axis helical gear reducers. Parallel-axis helical gear reducers align their shafts parallel to each other, providing efficient torque transmission and smoother operation, ideal for applications with limited space. In contrast, perpendicular-axis helical gear reducers align their shafts at right angles, delivering high-speed reduction and versatile mounting options, making them suitable for diverse industrial applications. Both types enhance performance and reliability in mechanical systems.</p></p>
