<p><ul><li>Lateral Flow Immunochromatographic Assay</li><li>Immunogold Assay</li><li>PCR-Enzyme Hybridization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rapid adenovirus testing market includes various types of assays. Lateral Flow Immunochromatographic Assays are simple, quick tests that use a sample to produce visible results, often in a portable format. Immunogold Assays utilize gold nanoparticles to enhance sensitivity and visualization of the target virus. PCR-Enzyme Hybridization Assays combine polymerase chain reaction for amplification with enzyme-based detection, offering high specificity and sensitivity. Each method caters to different diagnostic needs, emphasizing speed, accuracy, and ease of use.</p></p>
