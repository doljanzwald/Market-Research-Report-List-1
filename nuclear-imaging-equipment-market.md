<p><strong>The Nuclear Imaging Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SPECT</li><li>Hybrid PET</li><li>Planar Scintigraphy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nuclear imaging equipment encompasses various types, including SPECT (Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography), Hybrid PET (Positron Emission Tomography), and Planar Scintigraphy. SPECT provides 3D images for functional assessments of organs using gamma rays, making it valuable in diagnosing conditions like heart diseases. Hybrid PET combines metabolic data with anatomical information by merging PET and CT scans, enhancing overall diagnostic accuracy. Planar scintigraphy, a simpler technique, captures 2D images, primarily for evaluating organ health, particularly for thyroid and bone assessments.</p></p>
