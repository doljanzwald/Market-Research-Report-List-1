<p><p>Contact ultrasonic sensors are divided into two main types: open type and sealed type. Open type sensors are designed for environments with minimal contamination risks, allowing easy access for maintenance. They are adaptable for various applications but may be less durable against harsh conditions. Sealed type sensors, on the other hand, are built to withstand challenging environments, including exposure to moisture and dirt. This makes them ideal for industrial and outdoor applications where reliability and longevity are crucial.</p></p>
