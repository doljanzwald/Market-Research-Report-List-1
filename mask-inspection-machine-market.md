<p><strong>The Mask Inspection Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Inspection Machine market consists of two primary methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method inspects a mask by comparing it directly with a known good die, ensuring precision in defect detection. In contrast, the DB method utilizes a database of previously inspected masks to identify defects, facilitating rapid analysis. Both methods are essential for ensuring the quality of photomasks in semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing yields and maintaining production efficiency.</p></p>
