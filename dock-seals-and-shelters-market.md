<p><p>The dock seals and shelters market is categorized into three main types. Mechanical dock seals and shelters utilize rigid structures to create an airtight barrier, enhancing energy efficiency and protecting against weather elements. Inflatable dock seals and shelters feature flexible, inflatable designs that adapt to varying load sizes, providing a snug fit and superior insulation. Sponge dock seals and shelters employ compressible foam materials to create a seal, effectively minimizing air and water ingress while accommodating frequent loading and unloading.</p></p>
