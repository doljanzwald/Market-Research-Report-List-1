<p><p>Building Information Modeling (BIM) revolutionizes the construction industry by providing a digital representation of physical and functional characteristics of buildings. The market is categorized into three types: 3D BIM focuses on the management of design models, enabling visualizations and spatial coordination. 4D BIM incorporates scheduling, allowing for timeline visualization and project management. 5D BIM adds cost management, integrating budgeting and financial analysis. Together, these approaches enhance collaboration, efficiency, and decision-making throughout the construction lifecycle.</p></p>
