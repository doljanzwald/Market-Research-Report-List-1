<p><p>The Maleic Anhydride Grafted Polypropylene market is primarily segmented into two types: Homopolymer Type and Copolymer Type. The Homopolymer Type consists of polypropylene chains with maleic anhydride grafted onto them, enhancing properties like adhesion and compatibility with other materials. The Copolymer Type, on the other hand, combines polypropylene with other monomers, offering improved flexibility and strength. Both types cater to diverse applications in automotive, packaging, and construction, driven by a growing demand for advanced, multifunctional polymer materials.</p></p>
