<p><p>Online coaching platforms can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based platforms are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access coaching services from anywhere with an internet connection. This model offers flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT costs. On-premises solutions, on the other hand, are installed locally on a user's hardware, providing greater control over data and security. However, they require significant upfront investment and maintenance, making them less flexible than cloud-based options.</p></p>
