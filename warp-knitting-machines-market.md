<p><p>Warp knitting machines are categorized mainly into two types: Tricot machines and Raschel machines. Tricot machines produce lightweight, stretchable fabrics typically used in lingerie, sportswear, and linings, known for their smooth surface and versatility. On the other hand, Raschel machines create heavier, more textured fabrics suitable for curtains, lace, and industrial applications, often featuring intricate patterns and designs. Both types play crucial roles in the textile industry, catering to diverse manufacturing needs and preferences.</p></p>
