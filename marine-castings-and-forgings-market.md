<p><p>The Marine Castings and Forgings market comprises two primary segments: marine castings and marine forgings. Marine castings involve the process of pouring liquid metal into molds to create complex shapes used in shipbuilding and equipment manufacturing. This method allows for intricate designs and weight reduction. Marine forgings, on the other hand, involve shaping metal through compressive forces, resulting in strong and durable components. Both segments cater to the maritime industry’s demand for high-performance materials that withstand harsh marine conditions.</p></p>
