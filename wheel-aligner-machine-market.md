<p><strong>The Wheel Aligner Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Wheel Aligner</li><li>3D Wheel Aligner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheel aligner machine market features two primary types: CCD wheel aligners and 3D wheel aligners. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) wheel aligners use cameras to capture images of the vehicle's wheel angles, providing accurate alignment measurements. They are known for their reliability and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, 3D wheel aligners utilize advanced technology to measure wheel angles in three dimensions, offering enhanced precision and faster diagnostics. Both types cater to different user needs in automotive service and maintenance.</p></p>
