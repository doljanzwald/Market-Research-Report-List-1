<p><p>The BGA (Ball Grid Array) Inspection System market can be categorized into three main types: Electrical Testing, Optical or Visual Inspection, and X-Ray Inspection. Electrical Testing evaluates the functionality and integrity of BGA connections through electrical signals. Optical or Visual Inspection involves using cameras to detect surface defects or misalignments. X-Ray Inspection provides an internal view of joints and solder connections, enabling the detection of hidden issues. Together, these methods ensure quality control in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
