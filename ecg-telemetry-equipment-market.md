<p><p>ECG telemetry equipment encompasses various types of devices used for heart monitoring. Resting ECG devices are designed to capture the heart's electrical activity while the patient is at rest, providing a baseline for cardiac health. Stress ECG devices, on the other hand, monitor the heart during physical exertion, helping to assess cardiac performance under stress. Other market segments may include wireless ECG monitors, event recorders, and Holter monitors, which serve specific diagnostic and monitoring needs beyond traditional ECGs.</p></p>
