<p><p>The dairy fermentation starter market is categorized into three main types: mesophilic, thermophilic, and probiotic starters. Mesophilic starters ferment at moderate temperatures, ideal for products like yogurt and soft cheeses. Thermophilic starters thrive in higher temperatures, commonly used in making hard cheeses and yogurt. Probiotics are live beneficial bacteria that enhance gut health and are increasingly incorporated into dairy products, catering to health-conscious consumers. Each type contributes uniquely to flavor, texture, and nutritional value in dairy products.</p></p>
