<p><strong>The Patient Cooling Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Cooling Systems</li><li>Intravascular Cooling Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Patient Cooling Device market is primarily divided into Surface Cooling Systems and Intravascular Cooling Systems. Surface Cooling Systems use external mechanisms, like cooling blankets or pads, to reduce body temperature through conductive heat exchange. In contrast, Intravascular Cooling Systems involve the use of catheters to circulate cooled fluids directly into the bloodstream, offering precise temperature control. Both systems are crucial in clinical settings for managing conditions such as cardiac arrest, heat stroke, and during surgical procedures to minimize brain and organ damage.</p></p>
