<p><strong>The Automotive Rod Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Rod Antenna</li><li>Passive Rod Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive rod antenna market is divided into two main types: active rod antennas and passive rod antennas. Active rod antennas incorporate built-in amplification systems, enhancing signal reception and improving performance, especially in challenging environments. Conversely, passive rod antennas rely solely on external signals without amplification, making them simpler and more cost-effective, though potentially less effective in weak signal areas. Both types serve critical roles in vehicle communication, infotainment systems, and navigation, catering to diverse consumer needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
