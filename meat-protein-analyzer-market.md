<p><strong>The Meat Protein Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Meat Protein Analyzer market is primarily categorized into two technologies: DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy). DEXA technology utilizes X-ray measurements to assess the composition of meat, providing accurate data on protein content and fat levels. In contrast, NIR technology employs near-infrared light to analyze meat samples, offering rapid, non-destructive evaluation of protein and moisture levels. Both technologies are essential for ensuring quality control and nutritional assessment in the meat industry.</p></p>
