<p><strong>The Single Seat Rowboat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sculling Rowboat</li><li>Sweep Rowboat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single seat rowboat market comprises two main types: sculling and sweep rowboats. Sculling rowboats have two oars, allowing the rower to propel the boat efficiently and maintain greater maneuverability. They are typically used in competitive rowing and recreational activities. In contrast, sweep rowboats feature a single oar per rower, emphasizing teamwork as each rower uses a long oar from one side. This type is focused on coordinated rowing, commonly seen in team competitions and group outings.</p></p>
