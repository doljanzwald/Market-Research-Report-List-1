<p><strong>The Single-point Laser Vibrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Speckle</li><li>Laser Interference</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single-point Laser Vibrometer market is primarily divided into two types: Laser Speckle and Laser Interference. Laser Speckle technology utilizes the random interference pattern of scattered laser light to measure vibrations, offering high sensitivity and resolution. In contrast, Laser Interference employs coherent laser beams to detect minute displacements by analyzing phase changes between the beams, providing precise measurements in various applications. Both technologies are essential for non-contact vibration analysis in fields such as structural monitoring, materials testing, and mechanical diagnostics.</p></p>
