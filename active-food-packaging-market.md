<p><p>Active food packaging involves enhancing food preservation through various materials. PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) provides excellent barrier properties against gases and moisture, extending shelf life. EVOH (Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol) is valued for its superior oxygen barrier, preventing spoilage. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) offers strength and impact resistance, ideal for durable packaging. Composite materials combine multiple substances, delivering versatile properties tailored for specific food preservation needs. Together, these materials contribute to improved food safety, quality, and extended shelf life.</p></p>
