<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multiple Layers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tissue packaging market comprises two primary types: single-layer and multiple-layer packaging. Single-layer packaging involves a single sheet of material, providing basic protection and lightweight options for various products. In contrast, multiple-layer packaging combines two or more sheets, enhancing durability, moisture resistance, and insulation. This layered approach allows for improved product shelf life and aesthetic appeal. As consumer preferences evolve, both types find their niches, catering to different packaging needs across industries like food, healthcare, and personal care.</p></p>
