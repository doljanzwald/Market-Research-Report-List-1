<p><p>The capnography market comprises various types of devices essential for measuring carbon dioxide levels in exhaled breath. Main-stream capnographs feature sensors directly in the airway, providing immediate readings. Side-stream capnographs collect samples through a tube, allowing for flexibility in patient monitoring. Micro-stream capnographs utilize advanced technology for low-flow sampling, enhancing efficiency in various settings. Additionally, the capnography disposables market includes essential single-use components, such as sampling lines and sensors, ensuring hygienic practices and effective monitoring in clinical environments.</p></p>
