<p><p>The inflatable life vests market comprises two primary types: manual and auto inflatable life vests. Manual inflatable life vests require the wearer to pull a cord to activate inflation, providing a reliable option for those who prefer control over the deployment process. In contrast, auto inflatable life vests automatically inflate upon immersion in water, offering immediate buoyancy without user intervention. Both types cater to various safety needs in boating, emergency situations, and water sports, enhancing overall safety and confidence for users.</p></p>
