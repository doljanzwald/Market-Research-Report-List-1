Middle-end services focus on coding and claim management, optimizing reimbursement processes. Back-end services handle accounts receivable management, payment posting, and denial management, securing revenue recovery. Each segment plays a crucial role in streamlining the billing process, enhancing efficiency, and improving financial outcomes for healthcare providers.</p></p>
